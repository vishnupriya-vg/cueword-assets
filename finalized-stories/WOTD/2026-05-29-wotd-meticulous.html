METICULOUS
/meh-TIK-yuh-lus/ · adjective
paying close attention to every single detail
Level it up His LEGO sorting was neat meticulous — by color, size, and set number.
🎯 Today: tidy your desk properly — not shoved-to-the-side tidy. Every-thing-has-a-place tidy. That’s meticulous.
