“tele” = farAncient Greek root
Over 2,000 years ago the Greeks used tele for “far off.” English borrowed it to name machines that reach across a distance, adding a second root for what each one does:
tele + phone (sound) = sound from afar
tele + vision (sight) = seeing from afar
tele + scope (to look) = look far away
💪 One root unlocks dozens more: telepathy, teleport, telegram. Spot “tele” anywhere and you know: distance.
