MONUMENT.The sneaky fake — here’s why
MONUMENT comes from Latin monere — to remind. Monuments keep memories alive. Great word. Wrong root.
MONO  ·  Greek for “one”  👉
mono + logue (speech) = ONE voice doing all the talking
mono + tone (sound) = ONE flat pitch, no highs or lows
mono + arch (ruler) = ONE person in charge — ONE ruler
