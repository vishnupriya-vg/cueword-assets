“aud” = to hearAncient Latin root
The fake was AUDACIOUS — it comes from Latin audax (bold, daring), nothing to do with hearing. The other three all come from audire (to hear):
audible (able to be heard)
audience (people who listen)
auditorium (a place for hearing)
💪 One root unlocks dozens more: inaudible, auditory, audio. Spot “aud” anywhere and you know: sound.
