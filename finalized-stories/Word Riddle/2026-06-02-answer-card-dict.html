DISTRICT.the party crasher — here’s why
DISTRICT comes from Latin distringere — to stretch apart. It draws boundaries on a map. The other three? All about speaking.
DICT  ·  Latin for “to say”  👉
dictate — say it; someone writes it
predict — say what happens next
contradict — say the opposite right back
